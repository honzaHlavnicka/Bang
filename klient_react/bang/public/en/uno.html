What is the game about?

UNO is the worldwide king of family and party games. Forget classic cards – here, 108 special cards shining in four colors rule. The rules are similar to Prší, but thanks to action cards like "Draw 4" or "Reverse," a calm game can turn into total chaos in a second. The most important rule? Don't forget to shout UNO!

How to play? (Brief rules)

1. Starting position

Each player starts with 7 cards. One card is placed in the center, and the rest forms the draw pile.

2. Color on color, number on number

The player on turn must discard a card that matches either the color or the number/symbol of the top card on the table. If you don't have one, you draw a card.

3. Reporting the last card

At the moment you discard your second-to-last card and only one single card remains in your hand, you must shout "UNO!" out loud. If you forget and your opponents catch you, you get penalty cards!

Action Wildness

  • Draw 2 (+2): The next player immediately draws two cards and skips their turn.
  • Skip (Stop): The next player in order is completely skipped for this round.
  • Reverse: The game immediately changes direction (e.g., from clockwise to counter-clockwise).

Wild Trumps

  • Choose Color: This black card can be played on anything to choose a new game color.
  • Wild Card (+4): Not only do you choose the color, but the next unlucky soul draws 4 cards!
