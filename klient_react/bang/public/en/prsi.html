What is the game about?

Prší is the absolute foundation of every Czech table. You don't need any complex preparation – just 32 German cards and lightning-fast reflexes. The goal is simple: get rid of all cards in your hand before the others and with a winning smile, announce "Prší!".

How to play? (Brief rules)

1. Dealing cards

Each player starts with 4 cards. The rest of the deck remains in the center, and the top card is flipped face up as the foundation.

2. Discarding and Matching

You can only play a card if it has the same value (e.g., ten on a ten) or the same suit (e.g., hearts on hearts).

3. When you have no move

If you have no playable card in your hand, you must draw one card from the deck. Your turn then ends.

Cards that harm

  • Seven: The next player immediately draws 2 cards. If they also have a seven, they can play it, and the next player draws 4.
  • Ace: Stops the game. The next player skips one turn. They can only save themselves with another Ace.

Cards that help

  • Upper Jack (Changer): Can be played on any card. The player who plays it immediately chooses a new suit that must be played.
