What is the game about?

Dust, wild shootouts, and an uncompromising fight for survival. In this online version of the legendary game, you transform into one of the iconic characters of the Wild West. Your goal depends on the role fate secretly assigns you at the start. No one knows who is who – except for the Sheriff himself!

How to play? (Brief rules)

1. Deal roles and characters

Each player receives a secret role and a public character card, which determines the number of lives (bullets) and a unique superpower.

2. The game turn

On your turn, draw 2 cards. Then you can play any number of cards. Careful – you can usually only play one BANG! card per turn!

3. Distance and range

Initially, you only see neighbors to your left and right. To hit distant players, you must play a better weapon.

Who fights for the law?

  • Sheriff: The only public role. Must eliminate all Bandits and the Renegade.
  • Deputies: Help the Sheriff achieve his goal and protect him.

Who breaks the law?

  • Bandits: Their only goal is to shoot the Sheriff together.
  • Renegade: Plays for himself. Must be the last survivor.
